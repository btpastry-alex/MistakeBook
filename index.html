What is a “mistake notebook”?

A mistake notebook is where you collect the questions you got wrong (or were unsure about), write down why you missed them, and keep the correct approach in one place. It’s not about copying problems—it’s about turning every mistake into a point you’ll gain next time.

Why it works

Clarity

See your weak spots

Stop guessing. Your mistakes show exactly what to fix.

Efficiency

Review what matters

No need to reread everything—focus on the questions that actually cost you points.

Consistency

Remember longer

Review at the right time, and you’ll keep the knowledge for longer with less effort.

Spaced review: the real key

Memory fades fast. Spaced repetition strengthens it by bringing you back right before you forget. With a good rhythm—1–2 days, then 4–7 days, then longer—you spend less time but retain more.

Paper cut-and-paste is time-consuming

Traditional paper notebooks often get stuck at “making it”: printing, cutting, pasting, rewriting, and then struggling to find the right page later. It becomes a collection—rather than a tool.

MistakeBook turns that workflow into a few taps: capture fast, organize clearly, and review on schedule— so your time goes to improvement, not busywork.
