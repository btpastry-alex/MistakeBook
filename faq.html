Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Who is MistakeBook for?

For students and self-learners who want to organize mistakes, review repeatedly, and prepare for exams (school tests, standardized exams, certifications, etc.).

Q2: Can I import questions from my Photos library?

Yes. You can also take photos with the camera, then crop and annotate while adding them.

Q3: How does Spaced Review calculate my next review time?

MistakeBook calculates the next interval using “Base interval × Familiarity multiplier”, and schedules the reminder at 21:00 on that day (you can change the reminder time in Settings).

Base intervals (based on the review count after you save this time)
[1, 2, 4, 7, 15, 30] (after the 6th review, the base interval stays at 30)

Familiarity multipliers
1 → 0.7 ｜ 2 → 1.0 ｜ 3 → 1.6 ｜ 4 → 2.4

Formula
Next days = round( Base interval[review count] × Familiarity multiplier )
Then set the time to 21:00 on that day (editable in Settings)

Examples (assume today is 2026-02-11)

Example 1: 1st review, familiarity 1
1 × 0.7 = 0.7 → round = 1 day
Next: 2026-02-12 21:00

Example 2: 2nd review, familiarity 3
2 × 1.6 = 3.2 → round = 3 days
Next: 2026-02-14 21:00

Example 3: 4th review, familiarity 4
7 × 2.4 = 16.8 → round = 17 days
Next: 2026-02-28 21:00

Example 4: 7th review, familiarity 2
From the 7th review onward, the base interval is fixed at 30
30 × 1.0 = 30 days
Next: 2026-03-13 21:00

Two special cases
• Familiarity 5: Mark as completed, remove it from the review list, and stop scheduling next reviews.
• Familiarity 0: Do not recalculate the next time (keep the existing schedule).

Q4: Will my data be uploaded to a server?

No. Your data is stored on your device.
